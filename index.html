A wedding picture for her...

They say a picture is worth 1000 words, but they wouldn't all fit in the box on this webpage without looking funny. So I will sum it up: You were (and still are) worth the all the 600+ mile trips I made while we were apart, along with the new transmission, rear-end, transfer case, ignition switch, 2 plane rides, the towing charges, my mom calling the highway patrol, the false alarm DUI test, and a nerve-wracking ride with a crazy trucker. Here's to my bachelor party revenge, Kelly's hip flask, a beautiful wedding ceremony, and a slow convertible ride to a marvelous reception overlooking the city. Yep. I'd do it again.
